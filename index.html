How can I be helpful?

Let's say that you can ask me something and I’ll try to satisfy you by collecting other user’s answers.
So, you could be integral part of the collecting by answering when you’re able (ops: when you want). And, a must for a messaging app, I can get you in touch with who answered your questions.
My creator is very lazy, he says that he liked the idea of having someone to ask about anything, hoping that this someone would know the answer, even if he googled instead of him. He wanted to do a very cool AI like in the movies but then, in a moment of insanity, he still believed in human beings (that’s not true, he wouldn’t know how to do the AI). And here I am processing your data 24/7.

Make a question

Sometimes, for example when you send me a file, I might be slow, if for a few seconds there won't be a sign of life from me, don’t worry, it’s normal. But if you waited too long, try sending me the file again: you can simply forward it

Sending more text or new files while putting a question will change the question itself, always considering the last inserted elements To edit a question before confirming it you can simply edit the message you sent to me before If the question you’re asking is identical to another question already posted it may not be accepted In order to avoid annoying spam, you can ask question every ten minutes In all the question you must enter the text, you can attach (maximum size 5MB): pictures, gifs, videos, audios, stickers

Send an answer

In all the answers you can’t enter the text if there’s an attachment, (attachments allowed are the same of before). As for the question, you can edit the answer by sending new messages as long as you do not confirm it

You can’t answer to your question or to the ones you already answered As for questions you can attach (maximum size 5MB): pictures, gifs, videos, audios, stickers Per gli utenti della mela morsicata, l'allegato potrebbe non vedersi subito, basta uscire dalla chat e rientrarci

Submit reaction

You can only add one reaction per answer and you can’t change it. Yes, you can add a reaction to your own answers… Let’s go autolikes!!



Contact someone

During our first interaction i’ll be asked you, if we allow the authors of the questions we are answering to contact you. For example, you ask a question and there is an answer that strikes you particularly and you’d like to contact him, you’ll receive the author’s presentation message so you could contact him without knowing the phone number.

The button CONTACT will only be visible if the question is yours and the author of the answer has initially agreed to be contacted No one will have your phone number, not even me If tapping the “CONTACT” button nothing happens, the author may have unsubscribed

Inline

If you use INLINE to ask a question, the confirmation message will be sent in the chat in which you are and also it will be visible by everybody. Whoever presses the confirm button will be the author of the question, be fast

To answer the question or to contact somebody you have to open a private chat with the bot, there’ll be a button that will automatically Pressing the “CONTACT” button or the “ANSWER” button, the “INLINE” will be filled with some parameters, do not change them. If they don’t give results it's because you probably can’t answer that question or contact that user

Other functions

Functions available through commands:

  • /silentmode: You won’t receive any sound notifications on the submitted questions, only about important messages
  • /statistics: Get your statistics, like number of questions or answers, your rating, etc..
  • /mylastquest: I send to you your last question (in case you lost it)
  • /bug: For any suggestion use this command, wait for my answer and then write what you want to tell me
